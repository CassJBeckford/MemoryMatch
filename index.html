MEMORY MATCH


HOW TO PLAY


Memory Match is a game to test your memory.

Once the game has started the gameboard will feature a set amount of turns depending on the difficulty.

All cards contain a picture placed face down that will be revealed once pressed.

Each picture appears on a card exactly twice and in order to make a match you must turn over the cards with the same picture.

If you turn over a pair of cards that don't match they will both return to their face down position.

Number of turns remaining will decrease by one each time a pair of cards are mismatched.


EASY - You will have 15 turns to match all cards

MEDIUM - You will have 10 turns to match all cards

HARD - You will have 5 turns to match all cards
